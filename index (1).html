Specialty Services

Door Repairs, Hinges & Thresholds

Doors are one of the most used — and most neglected — parts of any commercial building. We handle everything from sticky hinges to full threshold replacements.

Door Repairs

Interior & exterior

  • Sticking, warped, or misaligned doors re-hung and squared
  • Hollow-core and solid-core door replacements
  • Panic bar and closer adjustments for ADA compliance
  • Weather-stripping replacement to improve insulation
  • Frame repairs for damaged or rotted door jambs

Hinge Services

Repair & replacement

  • Squeaky, loose, or rusted hinge replacement
  • Upgrade to heavy-duty commercial-grade hinges
  • Spring hinge installation for self-closing doors
  • Concealed hinge options for a clean, modern look
  • Hinge reinforcement on high-traffic entry points

Threshold Work

Safety & compliance

  • Worn or damaged threshold removal and replacement
  • ADA-compliant threshold installation (max ½″ height)
  • Adjustable thresholds for uneven floors or settling
  • Aluminum, brass, and rubber threshold options
  • Sealing and waterproofing for exterior entries
Why It Matters

Common Door Problems We Fix

Sagging Doors

Doors that drag on carpet or tile, caused by worn hinges or a shifted frame.

Failed Closers

Doors that slam shut or won't latch — a liability and compliance issue.

Drafty Entries

Gaps at the threshold let in dust, heat, and pests — driving up energy costs.

Trip Hazards

Cracked or raised thresholds create ADA violations and injury risk.
