Door Repairs, Hinges & Thresholds

Interior, exterior, and everything in between

Doors take more abuse than almost anything else in a commercial building. Between patients, staff, deliveries, and daily foot traffic, hinges wear out, thresholds crack, and frames shift. A sticking door isn't just annoying — it's a liability.

We repair and replace interior and exterior doors, upgrade worn-out hinges to commercial-grade hardware, and install ADA-compliant thresholds. Whether it's a single exam room door that won't latch or a full building's worth of threshold replacements, we handle it.

All work meets current ADA standards including maximum ½″ threshold height requirements and proper closer tension for accessibility compliance.

What We Cover

  • Sticking, warped, or misaligned doors re-hung and squared
  • Hollow-core and solid-core door replacements
  • Panic bar and closer adjustments
  • Weather-stripping replacement
  • Frame repairs for damaged or rotted door jambs
  • Squeaky, loose, or rusted hinge replacement
  • Heavy-duty commercial hinge upgrades
  • Spring hinge installation for self-closing doors
  • ADA-compliant threshold installation
  • Adjustable thresholds for uneven floors
  • Exterior threshold sealing and waterproofing

Flooring Repairs

Tile, vinyl, carpet, and transition strips

Cracked tiles, peeling vinyl, and loose carpet edges aren't just ugly — they're trip hazards and compliance issues. In medical facilities especially, flooring needs to be clean, flat, and safe.

We handle spot repairs and replacements for tile, LVP/LVT, sheet vinyl, and commercial carpet. We also fix transition strips between rooms, re-glue lifted seams, and replace damaged baseboards where the floor meets the wall.

For larger areas, we can coordinate full-section replacements during off-hours so your clinic doesn't miss a beat.

What We Cover

  • Cracked or chipped tile replacement
  • Loose or peeling vinyl re-adhesion
  • LVP/LVT plank replacement
  • Carpet seam repair and re-stretching
  • Transition strip installation between rooms
  • Baseboard repair and replacement
  • Subfloor patching for soft spots or water damage
  • Grout repair and resealing
  • Anti-slip treatment for high-traffic wet areas

General Handyman & Misc Repairs

The small stuff that adds up fast

Every facility has a running list of "small" things that never quite get fixed — a wobbly towel bar, a cabinet door that won't close, a wall anchor that pulled out. Individually they're minor. Together, they make your space look neglected.

Our monthly service visits are designed to knock out these items systematically. We bring a standard toolkit and materials so most small repairs get handled on the spot, no separate trip needed.

For anything bigger, we'll add it to your punch list with a clear scope and quote so there are no surprises.

What We Cover

  • Drywall patching and touch-up painting
  • Cabinet and drawer hardware repair
  • Towel bar, grab bar, and accessory mounting
  • Caulking and sealant replacement (sinks, counters, tubs)
  • Light fixture and switch plate replacement
  • Furniture assembly and anchoring
  • Shelving installation and repair
  • Window hardware and blinds repair
  • Signage mounting and holder installation
  • Weather-sealing for windows and vents
